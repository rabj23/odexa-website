About

Odoo people, since before we had a company.

Odexa started as hands-on Odoo work in January 2021 — implementations, consulting and support inside the Odoo ecosystem. In August 2023, that experience became a company of its own: one built to take on client work directly, end to end.

Journey

How we got here.

Jan 2021

Odoo work begins

Hands-on experience across Odoo implementations — consulting, configuration and support — builds the foundation everything else stands on.

2021 – 2023

Range widens

From functional consulting into development, server management and early AI-assisted workflows — learning the full stack around Odoo, not just the module.

Aug 2023

Odexa is founded

That experience becomes its own company — taking on client engagements directly, end to end, under one name.

Today

Full-stack Odoo partner

Consulting, development, infrastructure, AI and integrations for clients across Europe, the Middle East and Asia.

Goal

Be the last Odoo partner a client needs to hire.

Not a vendor for one module or one migration — a long-term technical partner who understands the business behind the system, and keeps it running as that business changes.

Inspiration

Odoo's own idea: modular, but whole.

Odoo's strength is modules that snap together into one system. We try to work the same way — consulting, code, servers and AI as separate disciplines that add up to a single, coherent operation.
